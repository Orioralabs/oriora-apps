# Desk Workday Log

Your Mac quietly remembers what you worked on — ask it anything, in plain English.

- **Platform:** macOS (Apple Silicon)
- **What it does:** automatically notes which files and apps you work in, then answers
  plain-English questions about your day, week, or month with grounded answers that cite
  your real files — plus a weekly digest. A global hotkey (Cmd+Shift+Space) lets you ask
  from anywhere.
- **Your data stays local:** your activity history lives in a database on your Mac, not on
  Oriora's servers. Only your question plus a small scoped slice is sent to the AI, billed
  to your Oriora wallet. It records file and app activity — not your keystrokes or screen.
