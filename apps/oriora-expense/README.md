# Oriora Expense

Snap a receipt, get a clean expense ledger and an audit trail — all on your Mac.

- **Platform:** macOS (Apple Silicon)
- **What it does:** capture receipts by photo (on-device OCR) or text, auto-categorize
  with AI, ask plain-English questions about your spending, flag anomalies, and export
  to QuickBooks / Xero / CSV.
- **Your data stays local:** receipts and records live in a database on your Mac, not on
  Oriora's servers. AI calls run through your Oriora account and wallet.
